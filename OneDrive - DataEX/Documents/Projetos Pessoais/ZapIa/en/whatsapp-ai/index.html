What is WhatsApp AI and why it matters

Artificial intelligence for WhatsApp means using language models (LLMs) — Claude, GPT-4, Gemini — to automate and enrich conversations on WhatsApp Business. Unlike traditional chatbots that follow predefined flows, generative AI interprets the customer's message in any format and responds contextually, considering the company's catalog, conversation history, business rules, and desired tone.

For SMBs, adopting WhatsApp AI solves four critical operational problems:

  • After-hours service: 30-50% of messages arrive after 6 PM, weekends or holidays. Without AI, those leads go cold.
  • Slow response at peak time: human team gets saturated. Customer waits 10+ minutes and gives up.
  • Manual lead qualification: team spends time on basic questions (price, hours, address) instead of closing sales.
  • Inconsistent answers: each agent responds differently. Well-configured AI standardizes communication.

No. We use Claude by Anthropic, better at instruction following, lower hallucination rate, supports prompt caching (-85% cost).

Not entirely. AI resolves 70-90% of interactions. Complex closing, serious complaints, strategic decisions stay with humans.

Hallucination risk?

Mitigated with: well-defined context, Claude's low hallucination rate, prompt engineering, human handoff. In tests, well-configured AI errs in < 5%.
