How WhatsApp AI works in practice

When a customer sends a message to your WhatsApp number, MercaBot intercepts it through the official API, sends it to the Claude AI model along with your business context, and returns a natural-language reply within seconds. The customer gets a real answer — price of a specific product, delivery time to their city, or your return policy — not "please select an option."

The AI also keeps track of conversation history, so when a customer follows up with "how much would shipping cost for that?" it remembers what "that" refers to. And when a conversation reaches a point that requires a human decision — a negotiation, a complaint or a complex request — the dashboard highlights it so your team can take over seamlessly.

Frequently asked questions — WhatsApp AI

What is WhatsApp AI and how does it work?

It's a large language model integrated with the WhatsApp Business API. MercaBot uses Claude (Anthropic) — you configure your business context once and the AI applies it to every conversation.

Is it compliant with Meta's policies?

Yes. MercaBot connects through the official WhatsApp Business API and follows all Meta messaging policies.

Can it handle complex questions?

Within your configured context, yes. For edge cases that need human judgment, it flags the conversation in the dashboard.

Does it replace my sales team?

No — it handles repetitive, predictable questions so your team focuses on decisions that require human judgment and closes deals.
