Why Claude makes the difference

It's not a script.
It's reasoning.

Conventional bots follow rules. Claude reasons about context, intent and domain. Same customer message — two completely different responses.

✕ Conventional bot (rules and scripts)
Claude · MercaBot (real reasoning)
Customer "Hi, I saw your ad but I can only pay next week. Is that still possible?"
"Hello! For payment information please visit our website or call our support line during business hours. We're available Mon–Fri 9am–6pm. Thank you for reaching out!"
✕ Redirected to another channel. The sale was lost before it even started.
"Hi, of course! 😊 You can pay in up to 12 installments by card or via bank transfer. If you'd like, I can send you a reminder on whichever day next week works best — just let me know."
✓ Understood the purchase intent behind the payment excuse. Closed the next step without redirecting.
Customer "I run a pizzeria and want to set up the bot. What hours do you recommend for customer service?"
"Configure your service hours in Settings › Hours. Please refer to our help manual for detailed instructions."
✕ Sent back to the manual. No useful answer delivered.
"For a pizzeria, what tends to work well: Mon–Thu 5pm–11pm, Fri–Sun 11am–midnight. Outside hours, the bot replies with an expected callback time. Want me to set that up as your starting point?"
✓ Knows real industry patterns. Delivered the practical answer and moved straight to action.
Customer "This is the second time my order has arrived late. I'm really frustrated."
"We're sorry for the inconvenience! Please contact our support team at 1-800-xxx-xxxx or support@company.com. Hours: Mon–Fri 8am–6pm."
✕ Redirected to a channel the customer has probably already tried.
"I understand — and this shouldn't happen once, let alone twice. Let me check your order status right now. Can you confirm your order number? I'm already notifying the team to prioritize your case."
✓ Acknowledged the frustration, took ownership and acted. No redirect, no form.

  • ✓Intent understanding — not just keyword matching
  • ✓Context memory — remembers what was said earlier in the conversation
  • ✓Domain knowledge — understands real business patterns
  • ✓Adaptive tone — formal or casual depending on the customer
