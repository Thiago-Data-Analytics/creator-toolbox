Why generative AI beats rule-based chatbots

Traditional WhatsApp chatbots present numbered menus and branch into fixed paths. If the customer's question doesn't fit the options, the conversation ends in frustration. MercaBot's generative AI takes the opposite approach: it reads the free-text message, understands what the customer needs and composes a reply using your actual business information — products, prices, delivery times and policies.

The result is a customer experience that feels like talking to a knowledgeable team member — even at 2 AM on a Sunday. And when the conversation requires a human decision, the dashboard flags it immediately so your team can step in at exactly the right moment.
